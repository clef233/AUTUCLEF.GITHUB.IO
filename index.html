Research Background: Based on U.S. Congressional debate corpora from 1995-2016, constructed a LoRA fine-tuned LLM framework for measuring political discourse polarization, analyzing ideological bias and temporal evolution in bipartisan political discourse.

核心方法：提出将微调后的 LLM 作为代理测量器的计算话语分析方法；结合基于上下文的 next-token 概率评分与生成式续写评估，测量语料潜在极化程度。

Core Methodology: Proposed a computational discourse analysis approach using fine-tuned LLM as a proxy measurement instrument; combined context-based next-token probability scoring with generative continuation evaluation to measure corpus polarization levels.

研究贡献：为政治极化研究提供新的计算测量思路，探索大语言模型在社会科学与计算语言学交叉领域的应用潜力。

Research Contribution: Provides new computational measurement approaches for political polarization research, exploring the potential of large language models at the intersection of social science and computational linguistics.

Isomorphism in Professional Esports Strategy: QCA Analysis of LoL Pro League Draft Data
科研项目 · 2025 - 至今
Research Project · 2025 - Present

研究背景：基于 LPL、LCK、LEC 2024 夏季赛比赛数据，结合组织社会学中的制度同构理论，分析职业战队 BP 策略同质化形成的多重机制。

Research Background: Based on LPL, LCK, and LEC 2024 Summer Split match data, combined with institutional isomorphism theory from organizational sociology, analyzed multiple mechanisms underlying the homogenization of professional team draft strategies.

数据构建：整合三个赛区的比赛数据，提取英雄 Ban/Pick、胜负结果等信息，构建版本依赖、高胜率队伍模仿、职业化标准化等条件变量；使用 Python 完成数据清洗、特征工程与可视化。

Data Construction: Integrated match data from three regional leagues, extracted champion Ban/Pick and match outcome information, constructing conditional variables including meta dependency, high-win-rate team imitation, and professional standardization; used Python for data cleaning, feature engineering, and visualization.

核心方法：基于 TF-IDF 与余弦相似度算法构建"BP 同质化分数"，量化队伍间策略相似程度；使用 Tosmana / fsQCA 识别导致高公式化 BP 的多条条件路径，解释职业战队为何在不同条件下趋向相似的 BP 选择。

Core Methodology: Built "Draft Homogenization Score" based on TF-IDF and cosine similarity algorithms to quantify strategic similarity between teams; used Tosmana/fsQCA to identify multiple conditional paths leading to high formulaic drafting, explaining why professional teams tend toward similar draft choices under different conditions.

研究意义：为理解版本平衡、赛事观赏性与战术多样性之间的关系提供数据支持与社会学解释。

Research Significance: Provides data support and sociological explanations for understanding the relationship between meta balance, tournament entertainment value, and tactical diversity.
