话语极化的计算测量 Computational Measurement of Discursive Polarization，llm based
研究项目 · 论文撰写中 Research · Paper in Progress
党派身份能预测多少政治语言？ How Much Political Speech Can Party Identity Predict?
用信息论的方法衡量美国国会的话语极化程度。对 1873—2024 年超过 1 亿条国会发言记录训练条件语言模型并进行分析，追踪其 150 年的历时变化。
Measuring discursive polarization in the U.S. Congress with information theory. Trained conditional language models on 100M+ congressional speech records (1873–2024) to quantify how much more predictable speech becomes when party identity is known — tracking the strength of partisan discursive boundaries over 150 years.
